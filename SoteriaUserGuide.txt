SOTERIA PERSONAL ALARM

Soteria is an Alarm system that sends a text message with the device's co-ordinates to cellphone.
This co-ordinate will be sent to the other device as a Google Maps link.

INSTRUCTIONS: 

1. Select the 'Configure Alarm' menu to set up the alarm. Make sure your location service is online.

2. At the next screen, enter your name, as well as the name of your Emergency Contact as well as their cellphone number.

3. The Alarm is now set, Go back to the first screen by pressing 'Go Back'

4. whenever the 'TRIGGER ALARM' button is pressed, the following message will be sent to your chosen emergency contact's device:
	"Attenton *CONTACT NAME*! and *YOUR NAME* in trouble. Last Known Location: *insert co-ordinates.""
